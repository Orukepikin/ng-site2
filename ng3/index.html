"The future is not waiting to be discovered. It is waiting to be built."

Neighbouring Genius is a civilisational software studio. We don't build apps — we build the substrate of what comes next. Our teams work at the intersection of neuroscience, quantum computing, synthetic biology, and artificial intelligence to create software systems that will define the next thousand years of human civilisation.


Founded in 2019 with a single conviction: the most important technology has not been built yet, and the people who will build it need a studio willing to think at civilisational scale. That studio is us.
