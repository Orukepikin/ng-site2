Origin
"The most important technology has not been built yet."

Neighbouring Genius was founded in 2019 with a conviction so simple it borders on naive, and so ambitious it borders on impossible: the software that will define the next thousand years of human civilisation does not yet exist, and the people who will build it deserve a studio that thinks at the same scale as their ambitions.


We don't build features. We build epochs. Our nine frontier disciplines — from neural interfaces to consciousness technology — are not service lines. They are our bets on what the next layer of reality will require. And we are building it, one system at a time.
